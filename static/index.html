General Wellbeing Tips

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule — aim for 7-9 hours per night
  • Stay physically active, even short walks can improve mood
  • Stay connected with friends and family, isolation worsens most mental health conditions
  • Limit alcohol and avoid using substances to cope with difficult emotions
  • Practice mindfulness or breathing exercises during stressful moments
  • Don't hesitate to reach out to a mental health professional — seeking help is a sign of strength

This tool provides a screening, not a diagnosis. Please consult a licensed mental health professional for a full evaluation.
